Photography

The museum of socks is built for those who are addicted to all kinds of socks. Here, you will have the chance to see and touch socks through various textiles, colors, lengths, and graphic patterns. And more, you can learn about the different history of socks and their special meaning or uses in multiple cultures. My visual strategy was to used dynamic colors and patterns to create the illustrations of these socks, to represent the variety and enjoyment of socks.
